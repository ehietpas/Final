Top Movies

Home Alone vs Elf

Home Alone: Lost in New York is about a boy that gets left behind by his family when they are going on vacation. Kevin ends up going to New York City. He has to scheme to stay in the Plaza Hotel. Also, he stops the 'Sticky Bandits'from robbing a toy store. It's a Christmas Classic.


Elf is about Buddy the elf who goes on a journey to meet his human dad. He eventually is accepted by the family and helps people believe in Santa.

To Compare


    Home Alone teaches some very valuable life skills, like how to stay in one of the most expensive NYC hotels without money and how to catch thieves from robbing a toy store at 10 years old.

But..
    Elf has an amazing soundtrack.

    Watching the Clean Bandits being outsmarted by a kid in Home Alone is hilarious.

But..
    Buddy the elf teaches us a new way to eat spagetti... WITH SYRUP

Elf Wins!!!!

Snow

Snow is magical. It is white fluffy flakes falling from the sky. What screams Christmas more than falling snow? Have you seen a Christmas movie without snow falling in at least one scene?


Christmas is just not the same with warm weather. Who wants to sing, "Let it snow, let it snow, let it snow"or "The weather outside is frightful..." when it is 70 degrees in Florida?


Skiing, Snowboarding, Snowmen, Snowball fights. I rest my case.
